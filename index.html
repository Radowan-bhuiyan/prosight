COMPANY BACKGROUND

Prosight Technical Services LLC was established in 2007. Prosight Technical Services (PTS) specializes in providing pioneering Mechanical, Electrical and Plumbing Services with organizational and technical efficiencies for ambitious construction projects across the UAE. PTS was launched with the vision of delivering a comprehensive range of services pertaining to MEP, Maintenance and Interior Designing.

By emplying the use of the latest and most advanced project management technology, PTS has continued to deliver superior solutions to all its clients. Using design thinking and lean project management, PTS is poised to provide innovative, cost-efficient and trailblazing support to all of our clients. In terms of quality assurance, PTS practices a rigorous testing and assessment process which ensures that only the highest quality work is being produced by our professionals. With a strategic framework, PTS with its services is working towards the objective of enhancing the efficiency of the work done by providing MEP as well as Interior design services under one roof.

Customer relationship management is of utmost importance to us and is therefore at the forefront of our interactions with the stakeholders involved. Striving to enhance the customer relations, PTS focuses on quality, timelines, health and safety as well as an anticipation of our customers needs. PTS has emerged in Dubai as one of the leading MEP service providers with a portfolio of highly successful projects and an impeccable team of high-functioning individuals.
